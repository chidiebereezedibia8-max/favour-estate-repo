Who We Are

A Tradition of Distinction

Founded in 2011 by visionary real estate entrepreneur Alexander Haven, Haven Homes began with a simple yet powerful belief: every client deserves a home that reflects their unique story.

What started as a boutique firm in New York City has grown into an internationally recognized luxury real estate brand. We've facilitated over $4.2 billion in property transactions across 45+ cities worldwide, earning the trust of discerning clients from every corner of the globe.

Our approach combines deep market intelligence with a concierge-level service model. We don't just sell properties—we curate lifestyles, matching extraordinary homes with extraordinary people.
